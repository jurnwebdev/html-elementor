Our Story

i-CR8 Designs Limited has been shaping Nigeria's built environment since 2012. What began as a passionate pursuit of creative architectural solutions was fully incorporated with the Corporate Affairs Commission on 22 December 2016 (RC 1381965), and has since grown into a multi-disciplinary firm spanning architecture, construction, facility management, furniture design, realty, and financial services.

Over the past 14+ years, we have delivered 200+ projects across residential, commercial, and institutional sectors — earning the trust of corporate clients, government agencies, and private individuals alike.

Our approach is simple: bridge the gap in Nigeria's property and construction industry by delivering quality affordable housing, professional project management, and superior customer service through every stage of the building process.
